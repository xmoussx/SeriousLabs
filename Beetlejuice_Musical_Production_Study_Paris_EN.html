üé≠ 2. Show Presentation

Synopsis

Lydia Deetz is a "strange and unusual" teenager obsessed with death since her mother passed away. When her father Charles, remarried to the eccentric Delia, moves into a house haunted by a recently deceased young couple (Barbara and Adam Maitland), Lydia's life takes a turn.

Enter Beetlejuice, a millennia-old demon with questionable humor and an iconic striped suit, invisible to the living unless someone says his name three times. He offers Lydia a deal: he will help her communicate with her late mother if she agrees to summon him. But can you trust a "bio-exorcist" with dubious intentions?

The musical explores universal themes with humor and sensitivity: grief, blended families, self-acceptance, and the need to be seen and heard.
